Who We Are

A Dehradun Story Brewed Over Five Years

Coffee Park started as a small corner in Rajpur Road with one La Marzocca machine, a handful of single-origin bags, and a deep stubbornness about not cutting corners. Five years on, we're still that same corner — just with a lot more familiar faces.

We source directly from roasters and farmers who care as much as we do. Our coffee changes with the harvest. Our food is built around what's growing nearby. Nothing on our menu is arbitrary.

"We didn't set out to build a café. We set out to build a place we'd actually want to spend time in."

The Space

A Colonial Bungalow Reimagined

Nestled behind a line of old deodars on Rajpur Road, our building is a restored 1940s colonial bungalow. The original stone walls, wide verandas, and wooden beams are still intact — we just let more light in and put better coffee in the kitchen.

The indoor-outdoor layout means you're never far from the sound of the wind through the pine trees or the smell of rain on the garden. We think that's as important as what's in your cup.

Restoration by Doon Heritage Conservation Studio
